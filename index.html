Course Overview

This course introduces the fundamentals of the Internet of Things (IoT), covering architecture, protocols, hardware platforms (Arduino, Raspberry Pi, ESP8266), and applications like smart cities and home automation. It includes 3 credits of theory (45 hours) and 1 credit of practicals (30 hours) over 15 weeks (Feb 26, 2025 - Jun 15, 2025). Prerequisites: Basic programming, networks, and hardware knowledge.
